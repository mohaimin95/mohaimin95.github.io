My Story

    Hello, I was born in a small town called Madukkur from Tamil Nadu. I keep on hearing words like everyone must have at-least a talent. I was so excited and wondering what could be mine. Some of my friends use to go to C++ class which is thought in my school on weekends. I wondered why these fellows are going to such classes and ruining their weekends and I just made fun of them. One day we were standing outside of the class for not doing our assignments. I enquired my friend regarding why you guys are going to school even on weekends and what is the C++ why you guys are learning it and what is the use of that such lot of questions came to my mind and asked him. He told that you can make software, games, website using that and he explained about his project which records of amount spend on milk in his home. It made me curious about programming languages.

I started referring to my textbooks for learning further and I did my first program which gets every subject mark and prints the total and cutoff marks of the given marks. My first program motivates me to learn further about programs. Which made me take B.Tech Information Technology at Aalim Muhammed College of Engineering, Chennai. In my college, My senior made a web app that stores and retrieves data about blood donors which made me curious to learn about web technologies (HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL). Later I felt not only the technical knowledge get me a job, but also the communication skills. I started watching Animated movies in English rather than watching in Tamil Dubbed. I decided to move to Bangalore to improvise my communication skills. I went to Bangalore on Sep 11th, 2017 attended an interview on the 13th and I joined a company on the 14th. I started learning new frameworks like Laravel, Angular thanks to my very small team. Then I learned NodeJS and MongoDB and became a MEAN Stack Developer and I learned to React JS and Vue JS Later.
