My Story

From Curiosity to Code

I hail from the quaint town of Madukkur in Tamil Nadu. Growing up, I often heard people say that everyone should possess at least one talent. This sparked a sense of curiosity and excitement within me, driving me to discover my own unique skill.

One day, I asked a friend why he sacrificed weekends for C++ classes. He showed me a simple project—tracking his family's milk expenses. That small utility app ignited a spark within me. Why just use software when I could build it?

Determined to learn, I wrote my first program to calculate exam cutoff marks. The thrill of seeing code solve a real-world problem hooked me instantly. This led me to pursue a B.Tech in Information Technology at Aalim Muhammed Salegh College of Engineering, Chennai.

Realizing that technical prowess wasn't enough, I worked hard on my communication skills—even watching animated movies in their original English audio! On September 11, 2017, I moved to Bangalore. By the 14th, I had joined my first company.

Since then, it's been a non-stop journey of learning. From mastering the MEAN stack to diving into Microservices and Cloud Architecture, I've evolved from a junior developer to a Lead Engineer. I love bridging the gap between complex backend logic and beautiful frontend experiences.

Currently, I'm focusing on Lead Engineering at Thoughtfocus, mentoring teams, and architecting scalable solutions.
